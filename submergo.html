Who We Are

Built for the reef


Team Submerge is a student-led ROV research group focused on coastal reef monitoring and environmental data collection. Our vehicle — Submergo — is custom-built for shallow-water survey operations.

We deploy along coastal reef systems, collecting high-resolution sensor data on water temperature, dissolved oxygen, salinity, and turbidity. Every dive documents reef health in detail.

Our mission feed updates in real time as we deploy, surface, and process data — bridging fieldwork and analysis in a single dashboard.
